About FruitCo.

Thanks for visiting our website in search for fresh fruits and juice. We are Fruit Corporation or simply FruitCo and our core business is to provide fresh fruits to our customers, refreshing fruit juices of various kinds and also distribute to various retail businesses our hand picked fruits. We are located in Nairobi and Mombasa county in Kenya.

We have various fruit parlours all over the country where we provide awesome refreshing fruit juices including cocktails and happy hour sessions. Our uniquely designed and branded parlours provide the best environment to have social meetups with friends and family. We also have offers on Mondays and Fridays.

# Products / Services

Fruit Parlours

Designed for social gatherings and friendly meeting places; our fruit parlours provide a cool and relaxed environment for enjoying nice evening with a family and friends. We have lots of great in-house made cocktails which are always on offer on Fridays from 2:00 pm to 10:00 pm and Sundays all day.

Fruit Shops

We sell our fruits on various outlets all over the country. We call the fruit shops but we also provide delivery services for clients buying via our online store. We sell only the best fresh fruits with great packaging following the latest packing standards. Go visit one of our fruit shops and get yourself a fruit to brighten your day.

Distribution services

Due to our huge stock of fruits from our splendid harvest, we offer fruit distribution services to retail vendors. We charge for the delivery services but also provide the option for self-delivery. Through use of technology we are able to perform qualitative logistics and delivery management.
